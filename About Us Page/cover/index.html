Who Are We?

Let Grow is a fun brand which offers people the opportunity to grow plants based on their own skill level. Every month, users are able to take a quiz which asks simple questions about their growing ability. Let Grow then uses these answers to send customers a unique plant specifically catered to their own skill level. Let Grow strives to provide quality service to all customers in a timely and professional manner.
